About Artem

Sports photographer & videographer, Seattle area

For the past three years I have been photographing and filming a variety of sports, with a primary focus on soccer. My goal is to capture the intensity, emotion, and key moments that define every game.

Based in the Seattle area and available throughout Washington State. Travel outside of Washington is also available for an additional fee.

I also have experience managing and creating content for sports-focused social media accounts — helping teams build their presence and tell their story online.
